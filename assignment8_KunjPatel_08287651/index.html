Introduction to Cricket

Cricket, a global passion, combines strategy and skill. Originating in England, it's played by two teams of eleven players. Its popularity transcends borders, captivating millions worldwide.

A Historical Perspective

With roots in the 16th century, cricket's journey from rural pastime to an international sport is marked by rule changes and iconic moments.

Rules and Gameplay

From Tests to T20s, cricket involves batting, bowling, and fielding, with each format offering unique strategies.
